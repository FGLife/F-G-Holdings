Working for FGL Holdings

We value the contributions our motivated employees bring to the company. We recruit talented and committed individuals to join our team, and we provide opportunities for personal and professional growth.


Headquartered in Caymen, our affiliates also have offices in Des Moines, Iowa and Baltimore, Maryland. Our employees are committed to partnering with our distributors to serve our customers.


For over 50 years, we have offered annuity and life insurance products to those who are seeking safety, protection and income solutions to help meet their needs.


We are proud to be an equal opportunity employer.
