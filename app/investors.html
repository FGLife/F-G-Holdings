We are now FGL Holdings

CF Corporation successfully completed merger with Fidelity & Guaranty Life. On [DATE], Fidelity & Guaranty Life (NYSE: FGL) announced completion of the acquisition of FGL by CF Corporation (CF Corp.) in accordance with the previously announced definitive merger agreement dated May 24, 2017. As a result of the merger, each outstanding share of FGL’s common stock has converted into the right to receive $31.10 per share in cash. FGL’s stock has ceased trading and will be delisted from the New York Stock Exchange. Continental Stock Transfer & Trust Company has been appointed paying agent with respect to the FGL common stock.
