Chris Littlefield

Chris Littlefield serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of FGL Holdings and Fidelity & Guaranty Life. As CEO, Chris sets the strategy for the company and oversees all aspects of the insurance business and operations. He joined the company in 2014 and formerly served as President and Chief Executive Officer of Aviva USA Corporation.

Dennis Vigneau

Dennis Vigneau manages all financial operations for the company, including; capital planning, SEC and regulatory financial reporting, investment accounting, reinsurance and treasury. Dennis joined Fidelity & Guaranty Life in 2013 with extensive finance and accounting with other major public insurance companies.

John O'Shaughnessy

John is leading our initiative to expand the F&G value proposition to additional markets. He has a broad background in development, design and product management. Prior to this initiative, he served as Chief Actuary and Chief Risk Officer of Fidelity & Guaranty Life. John has accumulated more than 30 years of experience with many of the top product providers.

Raj Krishnan

Raj Krishnan is responsible for all aspects of the company's investment portfolio. Raj joined FGL as Senior Vice President and Chief Investment Officer in 2008 and led the restructuring of the company's fixed income portfolio.

Eric Marhoun

Eric Marhoun oversees legal and compliance matters for FGL U.S. Holdings and its subsidiaries. Eric has more than 30 years of legal experience in US and non-US insurance markets. Eric joined the company in 2007 as Senior Vice President and General Counsel.

Wendy J.B. Young

Wendy J.B. Young oversees risk management efforts for the entire company and leads the internal audit function. Wendy joined FGL in 2000 as an actuary and has been involved in a broad range of actuarial and finance functions at the company. Wendy also involved in M&A activity for FGL.

John Phelps

John Phelps manages all aspects of life and annuity sales, product development, and distribution channels for Fidelity & Guaranty Life. John joined FGL in 2000. During his 30-year career in the industry, John has served as a personal producer, general agent and has served on the ACLI Life Insurance Committee, the LIMRA Distribution Leaders Round Table Committee and LIMRA Brokerage Committee.

Diana Hickert-Hill

Diana Hickert-Hill manages the company’s marketing and branding strategies including brand identity and reputation of FGL across key stakeholder audiences. She leads the investor relations efforts, and coordinates all internal and external communications, public relations and media relations. Diana is a seasoned executive in the insurance industry and joined FGL in 2017.

Rose Boehm

Rose Boehm is responsible for the company's human resources and talent management strategies and initiatives. Rose joined FGL in 2008. She has more than 25 years of domestic and international human resources experience in the financial services, manufacturing and government services industries.

Chris Fleming

Chris Fleming oversees operations and information technology for all FGL office locations. Chris joined Fidelity & Guaranty Life in 2011. Chris has a background leading change in insurance operations and technology functions and is a Six Sigma Master Black Belt

John Currier

John Currier manages the company’s actuarial function and leads the work on pricing, reserve valuation, modeling, life insurance and annuity product development, regulatory compliance and reinsurance relationships. John joined FGL in 2015 with over 25 years of insurance industry experience ranging from consultant to Chief Actuary.
