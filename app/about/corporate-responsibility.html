Corporate Responsibility

Fidelity & Guaranty Life strives to create a culture that rewards workplace engagement, teamwork and results. We focus our efforts on activities that benefit the long-term interests of our policyholders, shareholders, and the community. Fidelity & Guaranty Life along with its employees is 100% dedicated to giving back to the community through charitable donations and volunteer work. With the Employee Gift Matching Program and Corporate Charitable Contributions, the Company has supported over 50 charitable organizations such as:

  • American Red Cross
  • American Heart Association
  • Rebuilding Together (Baltimore)
  • Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation
  • Maryland Food Bank
  • Meals From The Heartland (Des Moines)
  • March of Dimes
  • Make-A-Wish-Iowa

In addition to financial support for the community, employees enjoy giving their time toward worthy causes in the cities where we work. The Baltimore community has participated in the annual “Rebuilding Together” project for many years now wherein employees and their families volunteer time and materials to refurbish Baltimore-area homes for needy families in the community. They have also assisted with serving lunch at the Helping Up Mission, which provides shelter and rehabilitation to homeless men in the city.


In Iowa, FGL employees have participated in volunteer events such as packing meals for Meals from the Heartland. These meals are shipped all over the world and are a key factor in fighting starvation and hunger.
