Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company

For more than 50 years, Fidelity & Guaranty Life has helped middle-income Americans prepare for retirement. We offer fixed annuity and life insurance products across the U.S. We partner with an established network of independent marketing organizations to distribute these products through independent agents.


With 700,000 policyholders counting on the features our annuity and life insurance products offer, we constantly push ourselves to innovate. We design our products and services to meet our clients changing needs, helping them to understand options and feel confident as they protect their family and future retirement.


Incorporated in 1959, Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company is headquartered in Des Moines, Iowa and operates in all states other than New York as a subsidiary of FGL Holdings, Bermuda. In New York, products are offered by Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company of New York.

F&G Re ltd

F&G Re provides Bermuda based reinsurance solutions for life and annuity.


F&G Re is a wholly owned subsidiary of FGL Holdings, domiciled in Bermuda. Its affiliates include the U.S.-based Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company.
